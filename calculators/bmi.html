What is a BMI Calculator and Why Use It?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using your height and weight to estimate body fat. This BMI Calculator helps you determine whether you fall into the Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese category within seconds.

Tracking your BMI is often the first step toward understanding your overall health status and identifying potential health risks associated with being underweight or overweight.

How Does This Calculator Work?

  1. Enter your weight in kilograms (kg).
  2. Enter your height in centimeters (cm).
  3. Click the Calculate BMI button.
  4. Your BMI score, category, healthy weight range, and additional health information will be displayed instantly.

Understanding Your BMI Score

For Adults (Age 18+) - WHO Standard Chart

This Body Mass Index Chart is widely used around the world for both men and women.

BMI Range (kg/m²) WHO Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal Range
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 – 34.9 Obese Class I
35.0 – 39.9 Obese Class II
40.0 or higher Obese Class III

For Asian Adults - WHO Recommended

Health risks can occur at a lower BMI for Asian populations. This chart is commonly recommended for India and other Asian countries.

BMI Range (kg/m²) Category for Asians
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 22.9 Normal
23.0 – 24.9 At Risk / Overweight
25.0 or higher Obese

Why is Checking BMI Important?

  • Assess Health Risks: A high or low BMI can indicate a higher risk of conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.
  • Set Fitness Goals: Use your BMI score to establish realistic weight-loss or weight-gain targets.
  • Track Your Progress: Regular BMI checks can help monitor the effectiveness of your diet and exercise plan.

BMI for Children and Teens (Ages 2–18)

For children and teenagers, BMI is age and gender-specific and is known as BMI-for-age percentile.

Instead of fixed BMI ranges, a child's BMI is compared against standardized growth charts.

Percentile Range Weight Category
Less than 5th percentile Underweight
5th to 85th percentile Healthy Weight
85th to 95th percentile Overweight
95th percentile or higher Obese

Important: BMI for children and teens is calculated differently. Please consult a pediatrician or healthcare professional for an accurate assessment.

Important Disclaimer

This BMI Calculator is intended for informational purposes only and should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice.

It is not a medical diagnostic tool.

BMI does not directly measure body fat and does not account for factors such as muscle mass, age, gender, body composition, or ethnicity.

Athletes and highly active individuals may have a higher BMI due to increased muscle mass rather than excess body fat.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making medical, nutrition, or fitness decisions based on BMI results.

A trained healthcare provider should perform appropriate health assessments to evaluate your health status and risks.

* Don't use this information to start a diet or exercise program without consulting a doctor first.
